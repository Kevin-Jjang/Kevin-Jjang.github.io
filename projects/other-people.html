Digital Magazine Production

Even though I have had access to the Forestry.io CMS to curate new posts for Collections, I prefer working directly in VSCode for efficiency. Forestry.io effectively allows the non-developers to create and manage front-matter. To provide more accessibility principles into their production, I added alt-text fields and instructed them where alt text goes.

New Digital Art Development

Another benefit of using a code-based website is that developers can create anything! I have implemented a slideshow function for the homepage to showcase featured posts from the current Collection.

In the upcoming year, I plan to develop dynamic, native interfaces for digital storytelling. Like manga readers, this would give users the chance to interact with the content and keep their focus on paragraphs at a time. The tone of the prose would be amplified by isolated text that pops out to the reader after every click through the dynamic reader.
