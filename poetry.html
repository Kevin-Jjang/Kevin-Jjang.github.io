Poetry

I have had an interest in poetry since I was eight, but I started writing my own poems in 2017. I write around seventy poems a year, where the metaphors bombard and mature my mind into a broader set of subjects and genres. Ironically, I do not write about coding as to separate the stressor with the destressor. As I carve an interdisciplinary pathway into computer science and writing during college, I wish to engage in digital poetics through the dynamic, accessible display of typographical layouts and dimensionality.

I do not submit prose in organizations I work at to reduce bias. Instead, I have an tradition of submitting to one unique organization per year.

This blog shall be updated with my own selected poetry and essays when I don't have school :)
